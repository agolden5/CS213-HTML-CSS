Studying Advice

Much of the advice people give about studying is wrong. Here are some study tips that are based on actual research.

Alternate study environment

Study in the library, in the coffee shop, in your room.

Varying the place you study helps you remember more

Mix content

Study a bit of this, a bit of that, some of this again, some of the other.

Switching content helps you remember more.

Space study sessions

Three one-hour sessions are more efficient than one three-hour session.

Self-testing

Test yourself or have a buddy do it. It helps you know how well you know.
